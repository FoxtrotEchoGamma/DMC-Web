The Centre

Dunstable Masonic Centre is a purpose-built building constructed in the 1980â€™s and currently hosts approximately 70 Masonic meetings per year.

Over the years the Centre has benefitted from many improvements to make it a welcoming place to visit. The Centre is served with a large car park with ample

parking for 46 cars.


The site has step-free access with the whole building easily accessible as it is all on one level, along with a wheelchair friendly toilet.


The Centre consists of three main areas:

Main Temple with seating for 66 members however it is able to accommodate up to 90 with additional seating.

A Committee Room which is used as the robing room and for small ad hoc meetings.

The dining room and licensed bar area of which can seat up to a maximum of 88 diners. The Centre has a resident caterer and staff who offer a wide choice of

menus and meal options to suit each meeting.

The dining room also has direct access to a secluded grassed area, behind the building.
